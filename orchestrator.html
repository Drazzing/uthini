Uthini Orchestrator

ECG software interface for Draeger terminals. Triage and move ECG files in hospitals—driving efficiency and improving patient care.

Overview

Orchestrator is an ECG software interface that connects Draeger devices to your hospital infrastructure. It displays ECG reports at the terminal for validation, saves time for floor staff, nursing practitioners, and cardiologists, and reduces patient identification errors.

The solution is designed for the high-impact environment that medical staff operate in—proven to work, easy to install, and built to interface directly with your existing systems. Delivering the correct ECG results to the medical team in a timely manner enables better patient outcomes.

Results

A hospital client in NSW, Australia reported a dramatic improvement in patient identification accuracy—reducing errors from 1 in 100 to just 1 in 7,000 ECGs performed.
